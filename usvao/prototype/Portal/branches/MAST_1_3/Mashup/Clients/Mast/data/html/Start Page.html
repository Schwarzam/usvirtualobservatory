Welcome to the MAST Portal

This website allows you to search various collections of astronomical resources by integrating resources in MAST and the Virtual Astronomical Observatory (VAO) in one place.


To begin, select the collection you wish to search, then enter search terms in the search box at the top right.

  • Objects from standard catalogs such as Messier and NGC will be resolved: M101 r=10s, NGC45
  • Common names often work: Antennae, Eta Carinae r=1d
  • Positions in RA/Dec: 9.0 -43.0 r=.2d, 0 45 r=30m
  • Sexagesimal coordinates delimited with spaces: 14 03 12.6 54 20 56.7
  • Galactic coordinates of the form g[lon][+/-lat] with no spaces: g102.0373+59.7711
