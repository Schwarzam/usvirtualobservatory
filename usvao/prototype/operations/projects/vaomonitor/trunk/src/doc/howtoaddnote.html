Adding a note to the Monitor Page

On the Monitor home page:

A. Click on VO Service Notices
B. Click on the "Add a Notice" link
C. Enter a message in the Message box
D. Choose an Effective Date and an Expiration Date.
*If no Expiration date is chosen, the default is two days after the Effective date
E. Submit the note. The note will be displayed on the Monitor home page.
