If you've used the Virtual Observatory, then you know that much of the data and capabilities can be accessed anonymously through public services and web sites with out any special logins; however, some features and services require more security.

This portal allows you to create and manage your own VAO Login--a single unique identity which you can use to log onto any VAO-compliant portal. Get one here!

Two-minute FAQ:

What is a Our Login
Also called your Our Identity, it is a user name and password that you can use to log into web portals in the VO.
Why create a Our Login?

Because your Our login is supported at a number of VO portals, you will now have fewer usernames and passwords to keep track of.

Your Our login name gives you added power while working in the VO through its single sign-on capabilities--once you're logged into the Our, you can move freely between portals without retyping you password. More importantly, portals and services can pass private data back and forth securely with each other on your behalf.

How do I get a Our Login?
You can create your login by filling out the Our registration form. We'll then send you a quick email confirmation which you must respond to so that we know you are contactable and not a robot. Once that is done, you'll set your username and password, and you're done.
