Which Chromospheric Binaries are also known EUV sources?

  1. Enter "Chrom" in registry
  2. Try the Search Me for first entry (short name "ChrAcBin")
  3. Click on the all sky search checkbox in the Simple Query form and search to get the entire catalog.
  4. Click on the Export button to export this to the inventory service.
  5. We can use the Inventory's default of 10" for the search so just click Find Datasets button.
  6. Look at the results. The Second EUVE catalog shows 30 matches, The WFC 2RE shows 9 matches
  7. You can view and correlate these results in the inventory or do further analysis in VIM.
